Welcome to Open Class Diagrammer | Craft Your Class Diagrams with Precision and Ease

Say goodbye to messy, static diagrams and welcome a seamless, dynamic way to design your system architectures. Open Class Diagrammer is your all-in-one solution for creating UML and Entity-Relationship diagrams that bring clarity to complex designs, making it easier than ever to visualize your data models, class structures, and their interconnections.


Why Choose Open Class Diagrammer?

  • Hybrid Diagramming for True Versatility

    Open Class Diagrammer combines the best of UML class diagrams with the power of ER diagrams, allowing you to capture both inheritance and associative relationships (one-to-one, one-to-many, and many-to-many) within a single, cohesive layout.

  • Intelligent Layout for Effortless Clarity

    Powered by a sophisticated force-directed layout, Open Class Diagrammer dynamically organizes your diagrams, ensuring that related classes and entities remain close, while unrelated ones stay out of the way. No more tangled connectors or overlapping classes—just clean, readable diagrams every time.

  • Precision Styling and Routing

    Customize each class to fit your style with extensive design options, from fonts and colors to borders and backgrounds. Plus, with orthogonal routing, all your connectors follow neat, right-angle paths that enhance readability, minimize visual clutter, and keep your design polished and professional.
